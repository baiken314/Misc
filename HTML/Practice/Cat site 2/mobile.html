Some cats

So we all know cats are great

And so for that reason I have decided to find a few cats that I think are quite praise-worthy. That isn't to say other cats aren't also praise-worthy- they are, but these ones were just lucky enough to be picked!

Grass Cat

This is a wonderful cat that, as any viewer can see, is in a field that is full of what is presumed to be the biological organism of grass. In the background, behind the focal point of the cat, a viewer can see a blurred, out-of-focus patch of trees.
